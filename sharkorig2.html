........RULES AND REGULATIONS........

1) The Pro Player Crew assumes that each participant who enter a tournament is aware of all the rules and checks them periodically . Insufficient knowledge of the rules can not be used as an excuse for breaking the rules.
2) Every participant who breaks a rule, will be punished. The exact way of punishment will be decided by the Admin involved.
3) Matches need to be played within the times otherwise the mat ch will be abondened.
4) If the opponent is not responding, report it to the admin before dead line.
5)Every player is responsible for his own connection and has to have a good internet connection to avoid possible ‘lag’.
6) time is 6 minutes.
7)The screen shot of the result should be published before 11:59pm.
8)Max effort should be taken to end the game in day time.
9)Decisions taken by the admin will be final.
10)In some special cases admin hae the complete right to re-write the rules.
11)Team strength should be less than 4300.
12)Maximum 3 legends is allowed.

UNEXPECTED CONNECTION LOSS.

1)If the leading player's connection is lost after 85 min of match time and have a lead of 2 goals or above the leading player is considered as the winner.
2)If the leading player's connection is lost after 75 min and having a lead than 4 goals the leading team will be considered as winner.
3)In all other cases contact admin for the final decision.
